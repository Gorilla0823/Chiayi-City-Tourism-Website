Song of the forest

The new landmark in the forestry corridor of Chiayi City - the song of the forest, is the work of the artist Wang Wenzhi, using the wood, rail, yellow vine and stone materials provided by the Forest Service to reproduce the glory of Chiayi's "wood capital".

Prison museum

The only in-situ preserved Pennsylvania-style prison building in the world - Chiayi Old Prison Chiayi Prison was completed in the 11th year of Taisho (1922). In the 36th year of the Republic of China, it was renamed as Taiwan’s Chiayi Prison, and moved to Lucao Township in March of 1983.

Chiayi Railway Art Village

Chiayi Railway Art Village was originally an idle railway warehouse. Under the planning of the government, the planning work of the “Railway Art Village” was started in September of the year, and it was changed into a venue for artistic creation and exhibition. The activities are leading, combining local characteristics and overall community building to achieve the ideal goal of the Railway Art Village.

The most lively and prestigious night market in Chiayi City is the Cultural Road Night Market!
